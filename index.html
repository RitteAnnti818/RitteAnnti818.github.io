Heeyeon Kim

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Artificial Intelligence at Jeonbuk National University (JBNU). I currently work as an undergraduate researcher at the Machine Intelligence Lab, advised by Prof. Sang Jun Lee, and previously at the Vision Intelligence Lab under Prof. Se-Ho Lee.

My research interests lie in 3D vision and neural rendering, including 3D reconstruction, monocular depth estimation, and 3D Gaussian Splatting. I am broadly interested in enabling machines to perceive and reconstruct the 3D world from limited visual input. Recently, I have been working on robust ground-plane modeling and unstructured-obstacle detection based on monocular depth estimation.
